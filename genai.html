Generative AI Capsule

Welcome to Generative AI Capsule: Master the Art and Science of AI Development!

Overview

Embark on a swift journey into the realm of General Artificial Intelligence with our Gen AI Capsule. This 4-5 week program demands minimal time and resources, providing an efficient path to mastery. Ideal for beginners, it covers fundamental concepts, enabling you to understand and apply AI principles swiftly. Dive into the foundations of AI and emerge ready to tackle real-world challenges in this dynamic field.

Eligibility

Enthusiasts and beginners keen on exploring the AI landscape are welcome. Whether you're a student curious about emerging technologies or a professional seeking to transition into AI-related roles, this capsule equips you with the essential skills and knowledge to thrive in the world of artificial intelligence.

Programming skills are not mandatory to apply for AI Lead certification.
