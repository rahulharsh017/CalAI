PRIVACY POLICY

Welcome to the official website of the California Artificial Intelligence Institute (CalAI®) accessible at www.calai.org. Your privacy is crucial to us, and this Privacy Policy governs our data practices and the information we collect from users in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) guidelines.

COLLECTION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION:

When you engage with us, we may collect various types of information, including:

Contact Information:

Such as your name, postal address, telephone number, and email address, provided during interactions with our products and services.Identity Verification Information: Such as images of government-issued identification (e.g., National ID Card, Driving License, Passport).Professional Interests and Experiences: Details shared through registration, surveys, or other online forms.Business and Marketing Information: Preferences and contact details used for communication.Transactional and Financial Information: Purchase history, billing details, and payment information.Technical Information: Data about your interactions with our website, device information, and IP addresses.Information from Third-party Services: Security data for authentication and fraud prevention purposes.

USE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION:

We utilize your personal information for legitimate business purposes: Fulfillment: To provide services, respond to queries, manage transactions, and communicate updates and offers.Security: Safeguarding our website, server, and technical assets, preventing illegal activity, and detecting fraud.Customization: Tailoring communications and content to your preferences.Business Improvement: Evaluating, operating, and enhancing our services and offerings.

DISCLOSURE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION:

We may disclose your personal information: To comply with legal obligations or respond to legal requests.To protect the rights, property, or safety of CalAI®, its employees, or users.To enforce compliance with our terms of service or payment agreements.To third parties in the context of business operations or service provision.

PROCESSING AND TRANSFER OF INFORMATION:

Your personal data collected by CalAI® may be shared, transferred, and processed in the United States, where privacy laws may differ from those in your country of residence or citizenship.

CONSENT AND WITHDRAWAL:

You have the right to withdraw consent and opt-out of specific data uses. You may unsubscribe from communications via www.calai.org/unsubscribe. We won't process your Personal Information after unsubscribing unless legally required.

UPDATING PERSONAL INFORMATION:

To update, review, restrict, correct, or delete your previously shared Personal Data, or obtain an electronic copy for transfer to another entity, please contact us at info@calai.org.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY:

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years. If you believe we collected information about a child, contact us at info@calai.org.

CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY:

We will update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. Continued use of our website after changes signifies your acceptance of the updated policy.


For any inquiries or concerns about this policy, contact us at info@calai.org.
