Certified Artificial Intelligence Developer

Certified Artificial Intelligence Developer (CAID TM)

Welcome to Certified Artificial Intelligence Developer: Master the Art and Science of AI Development!

Overview

Embark on a transformative journey into the heart of Artificial Intelligence with our AI Developer Certification program. Designed to meet the dynamic needs of the industry, this comprehensive course takes you from the fundamentals of AI to the intricacies of creating, training, and optimizing AI models on both labeled and unlabeled datasets. Elevate your expertise and become an AI Developer, equipped with the skills demanded by today's tech landscape.

Embark on a self-paced learning journey with AI Developer Certification, where cutting-edge knowledge meets practical application. Elevate your career as an AI Developer and be at the forefront of innovation in the ever-evolving field of Artificial Intelligence.

Eligibility

This certification is tailored for individuals with a strong technical background and a passion for AI. Proficiency in programming languages such as Python is recommended, ensuring participants can fully engage with the technical intricacies of AI development.

Programming skills are mandatory to apply for AI Lead certification.

Program Curriculum

Week 1 - Introduction to AI

  • Big Data and AI
  • Artificial Intelligence on the Cloud
  • AI in Banking
  • Chatbots
  • Exploring Feature Selection
  • Graphs and Graph Databases
  • White box XAI for AI Bias & Ethics

Week 2 - Essential ML

  • Machine Learning and Python
  • Supervised Learning: Classification and Regression
  • Unsupervised Learning: Detecting Patterns
  • Dimensionality Reduction
  • Visualising Data for Machine Learning

Week 3 - Deep Learning

  • TensorFlow and its functionalities
  • Deep Learning with TensorFlow on the Cloud
  • TensorFlow for Mobile and IOT
  • Life Cycle of Model Creation
  • Deep Learning with KERAS
  • Activation Function
  • Confusion Matrix
  • Underfitting and Overfitting

Week 4 - Natural Language Processing (NLP)

  • Natural Language Processing using Python
  • Transform Text File into Data Structures
  • Word Embeddings and Text Distance Metrics
  • Document, Sentence, and Character-Level Embeddings
  • Text Data Analysis

Week 5 - Large Language Model Operations (LLMops)

  • Foundations of LLMOps
  • Advanced LLMOps Techniques
  • LLMOps in Production Environments
  • LLM Prompt Engineering

Week 6 -Computer Vision & GANs

  • Computer Vision, Raspberry Pi
  • Multiclassifier to recognize traffic signals
  • GANs: Applications, Architecture, Variants and Advantages
  • Object Detection Using OpenCV
  • Face detection and recognition
  • Real-time Face, Age and Gender Detection
  • Image, Age and Gender Detection

Week 7 - Reinforcement Learning

  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Bellman Equation and Dynamic Programming
  • Monte Carlo (MC) Methods
  • Temporal Difference Learning
  • Multi-Armed Bandit(MAB) Problem

Week 8 - Deep Reinforcement Learning

  • Deep Q Network
  • Actor Critic and Policy Gradient
  • Method Learning DDPG, TD3 and SAC

Week 9 - Capstone Projects

  • Supervised-Learning Based Recommender Systems
  • Build a Live Chatbot
  • Cancer detection using computer vision
  • Speech Recognition System

Key Learning Outcomes

  1. Go from zero to hero in Python, Seaborn, Matplotlib, Scikit-Learn, SVM, unsupervised Machine Learning etc.
  2. Discover new ways to use LLMs, including how to build your own chatbot
  3. Use dense and recurrent neural networks, LSTMs, GRUs, and Siamese networks in TensorFlow and Trax to perform advanced sentiment analysis, text generation, named entity recognition, and to identify duplicate questions.
  4. Compare generative models, use FID method to assess GAN fidelity and diversity, learn to detect bias in GAN, and implement StyleGAN techniques
  5. Various ML modellings such as
    • Supervised Machine Learning Modelling.
    • Unsupervised Machine Learning Modelling.
    • Reinforcement Machine Learning Modelling.
    • Image Recognition and Computer Vision.
    • Build your own Recommendation Engine.
  6. Skills that employers desire, helping you ace machine learning interview questions and land your dream job.
